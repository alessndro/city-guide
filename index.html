Top three activities to do in Amsterdam

1. Culture and history

Amsterdam has a rich history and is home to many beautiful cultural landmarks. For example, you can visit the Rijksmuseum, the Van Gogh Museum, or the Anne Frank House to learn more about the city's art, history, and culture.

2. Biking and boating

msterdam is known for its bike-friendly culture and it's a great way to explore the city. You can rent a bike and cycle through the narrow streets and along the canals.

3. Nightlife scene

Amsterdam has a vibrant nightlife and a lively culinary scene with countless restaurants, bars, and clubs.
